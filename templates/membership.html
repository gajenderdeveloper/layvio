Still Got Questions? We've Got Answers!

What is Layvio Plus and What Membership Options Are Available?

Layvio Plus is a premium membership program designed for creative professionals who seek high-quality website templates, design resources, and ready-made website solutions. As a Layvio Plus member, you gain unlimited access to an extensive library, including templates, and more — all curated from top designers and studios worldwide to meet the highest standards of quality and usability.

Can I upgrade my Layvio Plus membership plan?

Yes, you can upgrade your Layvio Plus membership.
To do so, you’ll first need to cancel your existing subscription and wait for the current billing period to end. Once it expires, you can select and activate a new plan to upgrade your membership

How can I cancel my subscription?

Canceling your Layvio Plus membership is easy.
Simply click “Cancel Subscription”in your profile settings, then disable the recurring payment through your PayPal or bank account. This will ensure your subscription does not renew automatically.

Can I keep using the downloaded Plus resources after my subscription expires?

Once your membership is canceled or expires, your access to the Plus library will be revoked immediately.
In addition, the license for any resources you previously downloaded or saved will also expire, meaning you’ll no longer have the rights to use them after your subscription ends.

How do I contact Layvio for support?
