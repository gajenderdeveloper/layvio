Welcome to Layvio!

At Layvio.com, we believe that every individual and business should have the opportunity to establish a professional online presence.

Our mission is to eliminate the technical and financial barriers that often stand in the way of building a website.

With our ready-made templates and complete website solutions, we empower individuals and businesses to launch their online presence, sell products, and connect with global audiences. Whether you’re just getting started or have years of experience, we’re here to support you every step of the way.

Our Journey

We began our journey in the web hosting industry, where we quickly realized the process of building and managing a website was often complex, time-consuming, and costly. Traditional hosting solutions demanded technical expertise and weren’t always beginner-friendly.
Driven by the need for simplicity, Layvio.com was created — a platform designed to make website building accessible to everyone. No coding or design skills are required; everything you need to launch your online presence is built in. Our goal is to let you focus on what matters most: creating a website that truly reflects your brand.
As the demand for eCommerce grew, we responded by launching one of the only fully-featured, free, and commission-free online store builders. To further support our global community, we also introduced multilingual website capabilities — helping users connect with audiences around the world.
